# Relevance

--> The study is relevant to the proposed Women Wellness Application because it demonstrates the effectiveness of GPS-based emergency alert systems for enhancing women's safety. The real-time location-sharing and emergency notification mechanisms discussed in the paper provide a foundation for the SOS feature of the proposed system. However, unlike the reviewed study, the proposed application extends beyond safety by integrating menstrual health tracking, emotional wellness monitoring, and personalized recommendations within a single platform.

# Research Gap Identified

The paper reveals an important gap:

Existing solutions focus primarily on emergency response and location tracking but do not address other aspects of women's well-being such as reproductive health management and emotional wellness. Therefore, there is a need for an integrated platform that combines health, wellness, and safety services in a single application.
